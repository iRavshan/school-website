About Us

Welcome

Karshi PS is one of fourteen Presidential Schools created for the talented youngsters of Uzbekistan. The schools were established by Presidential Decree under the Cabinet of Ministers in 2019. Karshi PS opened its doors to grades Y5–10 in October 2021.

Vision

The vision of the school is to prepare the next generation of leaders to actively and positively contribute to the development of Uzbekistan by providing educational opportunities that encourage the nation’s gifted and talented youth to realise their potential as creative, innovative participants in a global society.

Mission

The mission of the Presidential Schools (PS) is to enhance the educational experience for gifted students through a programme of academic excellence and holistic learning opportunities to develop the leadership potential of its graduates to contribute to the future of Uzbekistan.

School Values

The values of a Presidential School graduate identifies the attributes of a student able to successfully meet the challenges of the programme’s vision and mission. The attributes serve as a clear visualisation of the ultimate goals of learning and teaching.

Academics

Admission

Prospective students typically need to submit an application form along with previous academic records or report cards to demonstrate their educational background. In addition, applicants are required to take an entrance examination to evaluate their academic skills and readiness for the appropriate grade level.

Graduation

At the end of the academic year, grade 9 students take IGCSE Level examinations, grade 10 students take the official AS Level examinations, and grade 11 students take A Level examinations. Students receive the National Certificate of Secondary Education and GCE AS & A Level certificates in 3 subjects.

Careers

At KPS, our Careers Department is vibrant and dedicated to guiding students in planning their futures. We provide personalized support to help students explore career options, prepare for higher education, and develop the skills needed for success beyond school.

Olympiads

This strong university acceptance record demonstrates our commitment to academic excellence and student development. We continue to support our students in reaching their goals, helping them transition confidently from school to college and beyond with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive.
